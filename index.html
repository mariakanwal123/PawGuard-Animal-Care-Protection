Our Story

Born from a Need for Real Women's Health Information

BloomWell was founded by Maria Kanwal — Medical SEO and AI Content Specialist at MedRankSEO — who was frustrated by the abundance of incomplete, confusing, and fear-mongering women's health content online.

Maria combines her expertise in medical SEO, AI-optimised content, and E-E-A-T compliance to create women's health resources that are not just informative, but truly trustworthy and search-optimised.

BloomWell serves readers across dozens of countries with content that respects women's intelligence and honours the complexity of female health across every life stage.
