Our Story

We Believe Every Animal Deserves a Chance

Founded in 2018 by a passionate group of veterinarians, activists, and animal lovers, PawGuard has grown from a small local rescue shelter into a globally recognized animal protection organization.

Our team of 340+ volunteers and 45 full-time professionals work tirelessly across 18 countries to rescue animals from dangerous situations, provide world-class medical care, and find them permanent loving homes.

In 2026, we're expanding our reach with new mobile veterinary units, an AI-powered animal tracking system, and partnerships with governments to strengthen animal welfare legislation worldwide.
